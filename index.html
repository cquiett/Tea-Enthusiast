Instructions:

The Tea Enthusiast is an App for tea lovers who want to search for books about topics relating to tea. Search terms such as "tea history" and "tea party" can be entered in the "Enter tea search here window". Press "Get tea infor" to reteive a result.

The result will return and display upto 5 books related to your search topic. If the book is available for purchase, the Retail Price will appear user can select the website Buy Link to purchase the book. Google Books is the search engine for this app.

The developer for this app has also provided additional links for the love of tea.
